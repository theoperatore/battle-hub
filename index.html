A Polar Clock

The polar clock is telling the time as the colors trace the circle.

Green -- denotes Seconds

Orange -- denotes Minutes

Yellow -- denotes Hours (24 Hr Clock)

Black -- denotes Milliseconds

More Really Awesome Content

Asteroids!

This demo shows off two forces of Physics: Gravitational and Electrostatic Forces!

All of the Red Particles are attracted toward the Grey Planet due to Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation.

To prevent this from happening, the Yellow Sun emits an Electrostatic force of repulsion.

Try moving the Sun around the demo area!

Animated Archimedes Spiral

Don't stare for too long! You may not be able to look away...

This cool design utilizes particles of different speeds all traveling in the same circle to make an Optical Illusion!

Polar Rose

Using the same idea as the last demo, the particles trace out a 24-pedal polar rose.

Keep watching to see really cool pattern emerge because of the varying speeds of the particles!
